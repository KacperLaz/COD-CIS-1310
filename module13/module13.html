BMW

BMW Bayerische Motoren Werke AG (BMW) is known for its blend of luxury, performance, and engineering precision. Founded in 1916 and headquartered in Munich, BMW’s lineup ranges from sporty sedans like the 3 Series, to reliable suvs like the X5 or high-performance M-models.

Mercedes-Benz

Mercedes-Benz, a division of Daimler AG was established in 1926, made a name for itself in the industry with luxury, comfort, and cutting-edge safety technology. Based in Stuttgart, the brand offers everything from executive sedans like the S-Class or SUVs like the GLE while also offering high-performance AMG variants.

Audi

Audi AG, part of the Volkswagen Group and headquartered in Ingolstadt, prides itself on “Vorsprung durch Technik” (“Progress through Technology”). Its car range includes sporty A-series sedans, spacious Q-series SUVs, and their high-performance RS models.

Porsche

Porsche AG, based in Stuttgart and founded in 1931, specializes in high-performance sports cars and SUVs. Iconic models like the 911 and Cayenne are symbols of Porsche’s focus on driving dynamics, engineering excellence, and motorsport heritage.
